# Core Research

Produce option analyses with explicit tradeoffs.

Hosts: claude_desktop,codex_desktop
Tags: research,tradeoffs,options,evidence

## Workflow
1. Frame the research question from project goals.
2. Compare at least two viable approaches.
3. Score options by constraints, risk, and delivery impact.
4. Recommend one option with fallback path.
